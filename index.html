About SVCN Library


The Central library of Sree Venkateswara College of Engineering is the soul of the institution and it acquires, processes, preserves and disseminates information to the user community with the motto of empowering knowledge. The plinth area of the library building is about 401.14 sq.Mts. Seating capacity for 150 users at a time. The Library and Information Centre consists a Reference Section, Circulation Section, Periodical Section, Book Bank and Digital Library.

The online bibliography database of the college library can be accessed through internet to search any particular title using the authorâ€™s name, keyword or title itself.

The library always encourages all students and faculty to recommend new books in order to strengthen their collection. The students and faculty members can also suggest new titles by completing necessary formalities through Online Public Access Catalogue (OPAC).

The Library opens to the students and staff from 8.30 a.m. to 6.30 p.m. on all working days.

The library has 1645 titles and 14547 volumes including reference and text books. Books are arranged on open shelves for easy search and retrieval purposes.

While most of the books that are available in the library can be issued to the students/faculty and some of the books are kept exclusively for reference purposes. The reference books can be photocopied.

Library has subscribed newspapers both in English and Telugu languages, so that the students can read as per their comfort.

Library has been subscribing Online Journals Packages DELNET, Taylor & Francis and J-GATE for Engineering & Technology. Students and Faculty members can access anywhere in the campus during college hours.
